Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10th June, 2025

Dhwani (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store personal data within our iOS app.

Data We Collect

  • Full Name: Used to personalize the app experience. Collected via in-app prompt. Stored locally on your device. Not shared or transmitted.
  • Camera Access: Used to scan documents and images. No data is uploaded or shared.
  • Photo Library Access: Allows you to select photos to create documents. All images are stored locally.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: Used for secure biometric authentication to lock and unlock documents on your device.
  • Contacts Access: Only your own contact card is accessed to display your profile photo (e.g., Memoji or Monogram). No contact data is stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit any personal data to servers or third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal information with any third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Dhwani is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data externally. All information entered or used in the app (such as name or photos) stays on the user’s device and is never shared or transmitted.

If you are a parent or guardian and have any concerns about your child's use of the app, please contact us at manasdevslopes@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the new terms.
